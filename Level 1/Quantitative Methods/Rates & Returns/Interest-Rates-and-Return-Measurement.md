# Interest Rates and Return Measurement  利率与回报衡量  

---
 
## 1️⃣ Three Ways to Interpret Interest Rates

**1. Required Rate of Return（必要回报率）**  
  The minimum return I need to feel this investment is worth the risk.

**2. Discount Rate（折现率）**  
  The rate I use to turn future money into today's money.

**3. Opportunity Cost（机会成本）**  
  The return I give up by choosing this option instead of the second best one.

---

## 2️⃣ The Constitution of Interest Rates  利率的组成要素  

$$
\begin{aligned}
\text{Investors' Anticipation} &= \text{Nominal Risk-Free Interest Rate} \\
&\quad + \text{Risk Premium}
\end{aligned}
$$

### 1. Nominal Risk-Free Interst Rate  名义无风险利率

$$
\begin{aligned}
\text{Nominal Risk-Free Interst Rate} &= \text{Real Risk-Free Interst Rate} \\
&\quad + \text{Inflation Premium}
\end{aligned}
$$

#### （1）Real Risk-Free Interest Rate  实际无风险利率
&emsp;&emsp;The return for postponing consumption when there is no inflation and no risk.

#### （2）Inflation Premium  通货膨胀溢价
&emsp;&emsp;Extra return to compensate for the loss of purchasing power caused by inflation.

### 2. Risk Premium 风险溢价补偿

#### （1）Default Risk Premium  违约风险溢价
&emsp;&emsp;Extra return for the possibility that the borrower may fail to pay.

#### （2）Liquidity Premium  流动性风险溢价
&emsp;&emsp;Extra return for holding an asset that cannot be easily or quickly sold at fair value.

#### （3）Maturity Premium  期限风险溢价
&emsp;&emsp;Extra return for bearing uncertainty over a longer investment horizon.

$$
\begin{aligned}
\text{Nominal Rate} = \  &\text{Real Risk-Free Rate} \\
&+ \text{Inflation Premium} \\
&+ \text{Default Risk Premium} \\
&+ \text{Liquidity Premium} \\
&+ \text{Maturity Risk Premium}
\end{aligned}
$$

## 3️⃣ Three Ways to Calculate Interest Rates

### 1. Holding Period Return (HPR)  持有期回报率  

&emsp;HPR is the percentage increase in the value of an investment over a given period

#### 📌 Formula

$$
\text{HPR} =
\frac{\text{Ending Value} - \text{Beginning Value} + \text{Cash Flow}}
{\text{Beginning Value}}
$$


#### Multi-Period HPR

$$
HPR_{total} =
[(1+R_1)(1+R_2)(1+R_3)] - 1
$$

### 2. Average Returns  

| Type | Formula | Application |
|------|----------|-------------|
| **Arithmetic Mean  算数平均收益率** | $\frac{\sum R_i}{n}$ | Include all values, including outliers |
| **Geometric Mean  几何平均收益率** | $\left(\prod (1+R_i)\right)^{1/n} - 1$ | Compound the rate of returns over multiple periods |
| **Harmonic Mean  调和平均收益率** | $\frac{n}{\sum (1/X_i)}$ | Calculating the average share cost from periodic purchases in a fixed money amount |
