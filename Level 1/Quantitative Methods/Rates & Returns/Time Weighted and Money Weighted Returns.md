# Time-weighted and Money-weighted Returns 

---

## 1️⃣ Money-weighted Return MWR 货币加权收益率
The money-weighted rate of return is defined as the IRR on a portfolio, taking into account all cash inflows and outflows.

$$
CF_0 + \frac{CF_1}{(1+MWR)} + \frac{CF_2}{(1+MWR)^2} + \dots + \frac{CF_N}{(1+MWR)^N} = 0 \quad \text{(NPV)}
$$

* **Note:** $CF_n$ is the cash inflows and outflows.

## 2️⃣ Time-weighted Return TWR 时间加权收益率
The time-weighted rate of return measures compound growth and is the rate at which $1 compounds over a specified performance horizon.

$$
TWR = [(1 + HPR_1) \times (1 + HPR_2) \times \dots \times (1 + HPR_n)]^{\frac{1}{n}} - 1
$$
